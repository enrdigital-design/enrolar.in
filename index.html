Our Journey

A story that started with
one student's dream

01
The Beginning

Born in the Heart of Guwahati

Starcweb Education Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular mission — to break the barriers between aspiring students in Northeast India and world-class universities abroad. One office, one dream.

02
The Growth

Enrolar Becomes a Trusted Name

As thousands of students found their path to global universities through Enrolar.in, we expanded — adding IELTS, TOEFL and GRE prep, finance guidance, a study portal, and a dedicated career platform.

03
The Evolution

Now Evolving into AbroadZone

Enrolar is proudly growing into AbroadZone.com — a bigger, bolder home for every student who wants to study abroad. The same trust and care, a new name, and a world of new possibilities.

A New Chapter

Enrolar is now
AbroadZone

We've grown beyond our beginning. AbroadZone is the next evolution of everything Enrolar stood for — more universities, more countries, more resources, and a team committed to making global education reachable for every Indian student.

Our study portal at study.enrolar.in continues to serve students as always, and will migrate to portal.abroadzone.com in due course.
